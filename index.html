Rules

How to Play

Simple enough for the car ride, strategic enough for game night.

1
Shuffle the Deck

Shuffle all 49 cards and place them face-down in the center. No starting hand — players take turns drawing one card at a time.

2
Collect Bunnies

Draw a colored bunny? Keep it! Bunnies automatically group by color in front of you. Collect 4 of the same color to win.

3
Discard Animal Cards

Drew a black & white farm animal? Bad luck — just discard it and end your turn. No effect, just filler in the deck.

4
Play Special Cards

Carrot, Fox, and Bunny Bond cards trigger actions. Save a Carrot for later — play it the next time you draw any action card!

5
Declare a Warren!

The first player to collect 4 same-color bunnies declares a Bunny Warren on their turn and wins the game!

🥕
Carrot Card (×6)

Steal one entire color group from your opponent. Or save it — you can play it the next time you draw any action card.

🦊
Fox Card (×1)

Plays immediately. Your opponent loses ALL their unprotected bunny groups. The rarest — and most feared — card in the deck.

💕
Bunny Bond (×2)

Requires 2+ bunnies of one color. Protects that group permanently from Carrot steals and Fox attacks. Play it early!

🐑
Animal Cards (×24)

Sheep, horses, cows, chickens, pigs, llamas. 4 of each. Draw one and your turn ends — just harmless filler cards.

About Us

Why We Make Games

At Play Central Games, we believe the best moments happen around a table — not a screen. We create card games that are genuinely fun for everyone, with no complicated rulebooks and no setup fuss. Just pure connection.

Bunny Warren started as a game we made for our own family. When we saw how much laughter and friendly chaos it sparked at every gathering, we knew we had to share it with the world. Every card has been designed with care, balancing just enough strategy to keep things exciting and just enough luck to let anyone win.

We're a small indie game studio with big ideas — and we're just getting started. Each of our games is a limited print run crafted for families who love to play together.
